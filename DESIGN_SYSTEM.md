# Design System

## Visual direction
- Premium SaaS aesthetic inspired by Stripe, Linear, and Vercel
- Calm spacing, strong hierarchy, and polished motion
- Light and dark themes with consistent contrast

## Color tokens
- Primary: #4f46e5
- Secondary: #06b6d4
- Success: #22c55e
- Warning: #f59e0b
- Danger: #ef4444

## Layout principles
- Use airy cards with rounded corners and soft shadows
- Maintain generous padding and clear section spacing
- Keep charts and actions aligned for quick scanning

## Motion
- Use short transitions of 220–300ms for hover and state changes
- Favor transform-based motion for fluid animation
